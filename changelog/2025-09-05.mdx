## Overview

This release adds **audio analysis**, a **global render queue**, **editor templates**, **dynamic text**, automatic **video scaling** prompts, and a clearer **workflow step** experience.

---

## What's new

### AI Video Templates
Create reusable templates for intros, outros, overlays, or brand elements. Build once; apply to any draft or remix.

<Info>
 **AI Video Templates** differ from templates in other tools in that they are understood and applied intelligently by Tessact AI. 
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to AI Video Templates">
    Go to **Settings → AI Video Templates** and create a new template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assemble the template">
    In the editor that opens, add assets from the **left panel**, arrange them on the **timeline**, and position them on the **canvas**. Add [dynamic text](#dynamic-text-in-the-editor) where needed.
    <Frame caption="Creating an AI video template">
       ![Creating an AI video template](/images/changelog/2025-09-05/ai-video-template-creation.jpg)
    </Frame>
    <Tip>
      You can use smart text to automatically populate text fields based on the **prompt** and the **content** of the video that the template is applied to.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the template">
    Click **Save Template**. In the modal, enter a **name**, **description**, and edit **template instructions** if needed.
    <Frame caption="Saving the template">
       ![Saving the template](/images/changelog/2025-09-05/save-template.jpg)
    </Frame>
    <Tip>
      You can edit instructions to make the template behave differently when applied using natural language.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply the template">
    Open any draft in the editor and select the new template in the list of AI Video Templates on the left sidebar **Templates** tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Dynamic text in the editor
Auto‑populate text fields based on the **prompt** and **draft content**. Inside templates, dynamic text adapts to each asset for consistent, on‑brand outputs.

Enable Dynamic text on any Text Layer (added by pressing <kbd>T</kbd> on the timeline) on the Properties panel on the right side. Once Dynamic Text is enabled, the content of that Text Layer will be automatically populated based on the **prompt** and **draft content**.

## Enhancements

- Auto video scaling — When a template’s aspect ratio differs from the draft, choose a **scaling preference**: **Fit**, **Fill**, or **Proportional**.
- Workflows — clearer step creation — The step‑creation flow is clearer and more structured, making step types and outcomes easier to understand.
- Added the ability to download transcripts in plain paragraph format.
