Use this catalogue as a checklist of everything Tessact delivers out of the box. Each section calls out the core feature and, where applicable, its AI-enhanced counterpart.

## Authentication

- **Single sign-on**: Delegate identity to your IdP so users authenticate with corporate credentials.
- **Two-factor authentication**: Require an additional verification factor for privileged or external accounts.

## Content Management

### Library & Asset Control

- **Multi-format ingest**: Upload and manage video, audio, graphics, documents, and canvas assets in the same workspace.
- **Metadata workspace**: Curate the fields and taxonomies your organisation depends on without touching code.
- **Metadata templates**: Define reusable templates and apply them to any batch of assets with one click.
- **Curated content browsing**: Spin up tailored players and collections for each platform or distribution use case.
- **Technical metadata**: Capture file-level properties automatically for QC and compliance teams.
- **Version control**: Track every cut, language dub, or delivery derived from the same master asset.
- **Dynamic watermarking**: Apply user- or team-based watermarks across any asset on demand.
- **Multi-view library**: Switch between five dedicated layouts (grid, table, storyboard, etc.) for faster discovery.
- **Metadata export**: Export metadata snapshots in CSV, Excel, or PDF whenever a spreadsheet is easier to share.

### AI Assist for Metadata

- **Content-aware AI indexing**: Extract context-specific insights; an episodic series is indexed differently from a feature film.
- **AI metadata autofill**: Populate synopsis, keywords, cast, and highlight fields automatically in 35+ languages.
- **AI metadata templates**: Blend manual fields with AI-generated entries so every deliverable ships on brand.
- **Prompted enrichment**: Provide custom prompts per field to steer how Tessact completes the metadata.
- **AI subtitle generation**: Produce captions in 35+ languages directly from the source asset.
- **AI thumbnail selection**: Let Tessact pick or generate the most engaging still for each surface.
- **AI-powered filters**: Describe the shot or storyline you need and auto-build filters while you browse.

## Review & Collaboration

- **Inline comments**: Annotate any frame from desktop or mobile and keep conversations organised.
- **Creative markers**: Draw directly on the video player for precise creative feedback.
- **Real-time notifications**: Subscribe to content-level alerts so nothing slips past your review cycle.
- **Approvals**: Track and manage status for every asset through delivery.
- **Comment exports**: Hand comment threads to finishing teams in CSV, Excel, PDF, Premiere Pro, or Resolve formats.
- **External share**: Invite external reviewers without issuing Tessact licences; feedback lands in the same thread.

## Video Player Experience

- **Multi-language subtitle overlays**: Display multiple subtitle tracks so localisation teams can review side by side.
- **Track selection controls**: Switch between audio, subtitle, and descriptive tracks without leaving the player.
- **Instant thumbnail capture**: Pull on-brand thumbnails straight from the player timeline.
- **Seven playback speeds**: Scrub content from slow motion to fast review with seven preset speeds.
- **Aspect ratio previews**: Preview each shot across aspect ratios before sending to downstream teams.
- **Real-time viewer tracking**: See who is watching and how they scrub via live seekbar analytics.

## Content Security

- **Identity-based watermarking**: Burn user ID and IP information into streams for tight security.
- **Two-click file locks**: Apply file-level permissions or locks in seconds.
- **Password-protected sharing**: Gate shared links with passwords to keep feedback loops private.
- **HLS-first playback**: Stream via HLS to prevent reviewers from downloading source files.

## Compliance Automation

- **Visual detection**: Flag compliance-sensitive imagery before it ships.
- **Audio detection**: Surface risky or brand-unsafe audio in 15+ languages, including Indian regional dialects.
- **Custom AI models**: Train Tessact on your policies and thresholds for precision compliance review.

## Live & Search Experiences

- **Live stream configuration**: Set up and manage live feeds from within Tessact.
- **Contextual AI search**: Ask questions in natural language and land on the exact clip you need.
- **Metadata AI search**: Query static metadata in plain language and let AI translate intent into filters.

## Workflow Automation

- **Workflow orchestration**: Build unlimited custom workflows to automate ingest-to-delivery steps.
- **Automated step logic**: Define conditional rules for when each workflow step executes.
- **AI-assisted orchestration**: Upload a workflow diagram or document and let Tessact draft the orchestration for you.
- **Chat-based workflow builder**: Describe the flow you need in chat and generate orchestration without touching nodes.

## Projects & AI Reframe

- **Project management**: Spin up unlimited projects that trigger relevant workflows and reviews.
- **Integrated review**: Keep approvals embedded as steps inside every project.
- **AI reframe**: Recompose any aspect ratio (9:16, 4:5, 1:1, 16:9) while tracking faces and key objects.
- **Split screen layouts**: Create multi-panel layouts that keep subjects in view.
- **Text spotlight**: Optimise text-heavy scenes with smart reframing presets.

## Tessact Editor & AI Remixes

- **Timeline & tracks**: Work on a multi-track timeline with full drag, trim, and split controls.
- **Waveform & volume control**: Adjust audio levels clip by clip using visual waveforms.
- **Fade controls**: Handle opacity and audio fades precisely at item boundaries.
- **Interactive canvas**: Move, resize, rotate, and align layers directly on the preview canvas.
- **Inspector panel**: Fine-tune X/Y coordinates, sizing, typography, and colours from a property panel.
- **Asset import & caching**: Bring in images, audio, video, and GIFs with local caching for smooth editing.
- **Undo/redo & copy-paste**: Iterate confidently with full history, duplication, and clipboard support.
- **Rendering & export**: Deliver MP4, WebM, and more with the codecs you need.

### AI-first editing

- **AI remixes**: Generate ready-to-publish reels or shorts in minutes, at any quantity.
- **Long-form generation**: Produce extended edits (micro dramas, episodic recaps, sports packages) automatically.
- **Smart prompts**: Receive AI-authored prompts that suggest compelling edits without rewatching footage.
- **Custom prompts**: Supply your own creative direction and let Tessact build the cut.
- **Chat interface**: Chat with your library to request edits or assets conversationally.

## AI Video Templates

- **Template orchestration**: Build unlimited packaging templates that govern intros, outros, subtitles, and graphics.
- **One-click template apply**: Apply a template to a new render and let AI adapt every element automatically.
- **Dynamic text**: Populate template text on the fly based on the source content context.
- **Captions & transcription**: Generate, edit, and export captions via integrated Whisper support.

## User Management & IAM

- **Roles & permissions**: Define granular roles that align with your organisation.
- **IAM assignments**: Apply permissions per user, group, or team.
- **Security groups**: Control access down to the individual file or folder level.
- **Teams**: Organise users into teams for scoped assignments and visibility.

### AI-enhanced metadata access

- **AI-indexed metadata**: Generate chapters, synopses, and scene-level insights automatically.
- **Elemental metadata**: Tag personalities, objects, emotions, locations, brands, keywords, and events frame by frame.
- **Contextual ads**: Let AI suggest the right advertising categories for each asset in your library.

## Notifications

- **In-app notifications**: Configure granular alerts for exactly the events you care about.
- **Email notifications**: Mirror the same controls via email for stakeholders who prefer inbox updates.

## Integrations & Transcoding

- **Adobe integrations**: Use the Premiere Pro panel or export Resolve-compatible EDLs straight from Tessact.
- **On-prem storage**: Connect on-premises storage so high-resolution operations stay behind your firewall.
- **Proxy generation**: Produce lightweight proxies in the cloud for remote collaboration.
- **HLS generation**: Spin up adaptive-bitrate HLS streams on demand for smooth previewing.
- **Rendering**: Render in the cloud or on premises using the delivery profiles your partners expect.

## Native Apps

- **Desktop support**: Access Tessact via native macOS and Windows applications, in addition to the web experience.
